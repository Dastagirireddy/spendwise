Why SpendWise

Everything your household needs

Designed for real life — not for financial gurus. Just open it, enter your expenses, and see where your money goes.

Fixed vs Dynamic Budget

Set your fixed bills first — rent, EMIs, subscriptions. What remains is your flexible budget for the month.

Daily Spending Tracker

Log every purchase with a category and note. See your daily total and weekly patterns at a glance.

Month-End Reports

Beautiful summaries of how you spent vs planned. Export as PDF or CSV for your records.

100% Offline & Private

Your data never leaves your device. No cloud sync, no account needed, no bank credentials ever.

Savings Goals

Set a savings target for the month. SpendWise shows exactly how much you have left after all planned spending.

Multi-Profile Support

Manage budgets for multiple people or months in one app. Switch between profiles in one click.
